    Review My Paper

    By developing a material loan and return registration system, the school's various assets can be effectively managed and monitored. This paper presents a feasible prototype of a database management system, covering the system's main functionality and design considerations. First, the system requirements were analyzed and the corresponding entity relationship diagram was completed. Next, the implementation of data privacy and important SQL instructions for providing statistical information are discussed. Finally, this article provides specific instructions on the testing and evaluation of system prototypes, including the advantages and disadvantages of database design, the concept of relational databases, and important issues such as data verification and validity testing. The entire project strives to provide a practical material loan and return registration system solution for the middle school student union.
